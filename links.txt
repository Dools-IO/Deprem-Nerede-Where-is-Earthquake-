# Deprem Veri Kaynakları (Earthquake Data Sources)

## Avrupa ve Akdeniz Bölgesi (European-Mediterranean Region)

### EMSC (European-Mediterranean Seismological Centre)
- Ana Sayfa: https://www.emsc-csem.org
- Gerçek Zamanlı Portal: https://www.seismicportal.eu/realtime.html
- API Endpoint: https://www.seismicportal.eu/fdsnws/event/1/query

### KOERI (Kandilli Observatory and Earthquake Research Institute)
- Ana Sayfa: http://www.koeri.boun.edu.tr
- Son Depremler: http://www.koeri.boun.edu.tr/scripts/lst0.asp

### ESM (European Strong-Motion Database)
- Ana Sayfa: https://esm-db.eu
- API Endpoint: https://esm-db.eu/fdsnws/event/1/query

## Amerika Birleşik Devletleri (United States)

### USGS (United States Geological Survey)
- Ana Sayfa: https://www.usgs.gov/programs/earthquake-hazards
- GeoJSON Feed: https://earthquake.usgs.gov/earthquakes/feed/v1.0/summary/all_month.geojson
- Harita: https://earthquake.usgs.gov/earthquakes/map

### IRIS (Incorporated Research Institutions for Seismology)
- Ana Sayfa: https://www.iris.edu
- Sismik Monitör: https://www.iris.edu/app/seismic-monitor
- API Endpoint: http://service.iris.edu/fdsnws/event/1/query

## Asya-Pasifik Bölgesi (Asia-Pacific Region)

### JMA (Japan Meteorological Agency)
- Ana Sayfa: https://www.jma.go.jp
- Deprem Bilgileri: https://www.data.jma.go.jp/multi/quake/index.html?lang=en
- JSON Feed: https://www.jma.go.jp/bosai/quake/data/list.json

### GeoNet NZ (New Zealand)
- Ana Sayfa: https://www.geonet.org.nz
- Son Depremler: https://www.geonet.org.nz/earthquake/unnoticeable
- API: https://api.geonet.org.nz

### Geoscience Australia
- Ana Sayfa: https://earthquakes.ga.gov.au
- WFS API: https://earthquakes.ga.gov.au/geoserver/earthquakes/wfs

## Latin Amerika (Latin America)

### SSN México (Servicio Sismológico Nacional)
- Ana Sayfa: http://www.ssn.unam.mx
- İngilizce Portal: http://www.ssn.unam.mx/english/seismicity/latest-earthquakes
- RSS Feed: http://www.ssn.unam.mx/rss/ultimos-sismos.xml

### INGV (Istituto Nazionale di Geofisica e Vulcanologia)
- Ana Sayfa: https://terremoti.ingv.it
- İngilizce Portal: https://terremoti.ingv.it/?lang=en
https://webservices.ingv.it/fdsnws/event/1/query?starttime=2024-04-20&endtime=2024-04-27&minmag=2&format=json
